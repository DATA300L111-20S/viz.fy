Viz.fy: The Story

Viz.fy was created as an exploration of Spotify's top 200 tracks in the U.S. by 2 music fans curious about the publicly available data to basic Spotify users.

The visualizations contained in this site are representative of what we thought might seem interesting to a music lovers, writers, and producers. This data is live and is updated every 24 hours with the newest top 200 tracks. Maybe one day we will really be able to see "what makes a top song."

Viz.fy was built utilizing Python3 and Flask as the backend, the front-end was built with d3.js, and JQuery.
